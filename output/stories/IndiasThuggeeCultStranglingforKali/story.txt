Story:
[mysterious] What if I told you that for centuries, a secret society in India was strangling travelers in the name of the goddess Kali? The Thuggees, operating from the 17th to the 19th centuries, murdered an estimated 2 million people. They lured victims with charm, only to offer them as sacrifices to their dark deity. British officers, led by William Sleeman, cracked down on this cult in the 1830s, finally ending their reign of terror. Can you imagine such a deadly devotion?
