Story:
Imagine a nation united by song! From 1987 to 1991, Estonians gathered in vast choirs, their voices echoing the longing for independence from the Soviet Union. These 'Singing Revolution' events transformed music into a powerful tool for peaceful resistance, fostering a sense of national identity. As thousands sang patriotic songs, they sparked hope and determination, ultimately leading to Estonia's restored independence. This remarkable journey showcases how harmony can create profound change, proving that music truly has the power to unite and inspire.
