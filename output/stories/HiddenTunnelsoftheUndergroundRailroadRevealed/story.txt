[mysterious] What if I told you there were secret tunnels weaving through America? In the 19th century, abolitionists like Harriet Tubman used these hidden passages to guide over 70 enslaved individuals to freedom each trip. With multiple safe houses, like the one in Philadelphia, these intricate networks were crucial for escaping bondage. Many tunnels were cleverly disguised beneath ordinary homes, making them invisible to pursuers. These remarkable efforts were pivotal in the fight against slavery, showing just how far people went for freedom. Can you imagine these hidden paths still out there today?
